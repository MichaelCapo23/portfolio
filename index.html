Learn About Michael

My name is Michael Capobianco, and I am a web developer with a passion for creating, worthwhile products that benefit the lives of those who use them. Whether it was during school or at work, learning the latest technology is something that has always peaked my interest. I got myself started in the tech industry by working at Coastline Micro. While at Coastline Micro my job was to integrate desktops and servers. This included system prepping for specific businesses and troubleshooting various issues brought before me. As I continued with my daily tasks. The more I worked I noticed myself trying to find the best way to accomplish any given task. This included taking the proper steps to find the component that is failing, finding patterns to integrate a system the quickest while making it look the best i can, and looking for the best usage of my time at any given moment. I immediately noticed this way of thinking can be easily transferrable to the programming courses I was taking online. That’s when I decided to further my programming knowledge by attending Irvine Valley College. While I was at Irvine Valley College I took a variety of programming courses such as Python, HTML/CSS, Java and C. As I took more and more programming classes, I quickly realized this was something I wanted to do the rest of my life. That’s when I decided to make the jump and attend LearningFuze. During my time at LearningFuze I learned best coding practices in Full-Stack Development using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Git, jQuery, React with Redux, Node.js, PHP and MySQL. Not only did I learn how to code, but LearningFuze also taught me how to work in a group environment. I can say without a doubt my time at LearningFuze was challenging, but I loved every second of it. I am absolutely ecstatic to begin my programming career and can’t wait to see where my career takes me. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions and I will be more than happy to answer them.
