Learn About Michael

My name is Michael Capobianco, and i am a web developer with a passion for creating, worthwhile products that benefit the lives of those who use them. I've spent most of my life looking for a career i can be proud of, i always knew it would be in the tech industry, which is why for three years i worked for a company named Coastline Micro, while attending Irvine Valley College taking a verity of programming courses such as Python, HTML/CSS, Java and C. I immersed myself in the latest technologies both at work and at school. While at work my job was integrating desktops and servers. This included loading the desired OS, updating drivers, system prepping for specific businesses and troubleshooting various issues brought before me. The more I worked I noticed myself trying to find the best way to accomplish any given task. This included taking the proper steps to find the component that is failing, finding patterns to integrate a system the quickest while making it look the best i can, and looking for the best usage of my time at any given moment. I immediately noticed this way of thinking transferred over to how i handled problem solving in the programming courses I was taking. The more programming classes i took the more i enjoyed programming overall, which is what ultimately lead me to join the LearningFuze program. I aimed to test myself and my love for programming. With my time at LearningFuze I learned best coding practices in Full-Stack Development using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Git, jQuery, React with Redux, Node.js, PHP and MySQL. I learned a lot about myself while attending the program, and none more valuable than confirming to myself this is career I want for myself. I can say without a doubt my time at LearningFuze was the most difficult yet joyful time of my life. I can't wait to hit the ground running wherever life takes me, feel free to contact me with any questions about my experiences thank you for reading.
